A-propos

Hello, nice to meet you! I'm Clément, a student passionate about applying mathematics to real-world problems, especially in the fields of Numerical Analysis and Artificial Intelligence.

I earned my bachelor's degree in Fundamental Mathematics from the Université d'Abomey-Calavi in 2019, followed by a master's degree in Fundamental Mathematics from the Institut de Mathématiques et de Sciences Physiques in Benin in 2022, with a master's thesis focusing on Numerical Analysis. In 2023, I embarked on my PhD journey in Numerical Analysis at the same institute, where I'm dedicated to studying and simulating groundwater flow in Karst aquifers.

Currently, I'm pursuing studies in AI for Sciences at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences in South Africa.

My research interests span across various domains, including Natural Language Processing, Computer Vision, Numerical Analysis, Probability Theory, and Statistics. I'm excited about exploring the intersections of these areas to solve complex challenges and contribute to scientific advancements.
