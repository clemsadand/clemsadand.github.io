Hello! I'm Clément, a passionate researcher and student dedicated to applying mathematics to solve real-world problems, particularly in the areas of Numerical Analysis and Artificial Intelligence.

I earned my bachelor's degree in Fundamental Mathematics from the Université d'Abomey-Calavi in 2019 and followed it with a master's degree in Fundamental Mathematics from the Institut de Mathématiques et de Sciences Physiques in 2022, where I focused on Numerical Analysis. In 2024, I expanded my expertise by completing a master's degree in AI for Science, fully funded by Google DeepMind, at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences in South Africa. Currently, I'm working on several projects, also as freelance at Upwork and leading several practical activities related to computer science and programming.
